Bengaluru's Tech and Economy and:
Bengaluru city is often called the "Silicon Valley of India" is the nation's premier technology and startup hub.
The city hosts numerous MNC corporations and contributes a major portion to India's IT exports annually.
Its dynamic economy attracts a vast population of skilled professionals from across the country,
driving rapid urban expansion.
Culture, Green Spaces, and Infrastructure:
Despite its modern image, Bengaluru city retains a strong cultural heritage,
evident in landmarks like the Bengaluru Palace and Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace.
The city famous green spaces, such as Cubbon Park and Lal Bagh, serve as vital lungs in the city.
